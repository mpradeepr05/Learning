LET 410

Versatile, easy to handle and with low operating and maintenance costs; the LET 410 is an all-metal, high-wing twin turboprop commuter. Designed to transport up to 19 passengers, and able to be modified to suit cargo missions and special missions for recreation – the LET 410 is currently operating in more than 50 countries on five continents.

The L 410NG is currently used by commercial airline operators as well as the government agencies and aero-clubs worldwide. Entering the Indian aviation sector for its unique features will be another feather, be it – for standard passenger transport, for cargo, for VIPs, as an air ambulance service, for sky diving, for even photogrammetric or patrol versions.

Some technical details:
  • Cruise speed: 405 km/h
  • Range: 510 km
  • Capacity: 19 passengers
  • Length: 14.42 m
  • Wingspan: 19.98 m
  • Height: 5.97 m
  • Max. Take-off weight: 6,600 kg
