About Us

We provide premier flying training in India. Our fleet consists of simple and complex Single Engine Airplanes and complex Multi Engine Airplanes with cross country flying to major International Airports. We provide exposure to our students to the modern radar environment as well as advanced instruments flying practices. The complete course runs in accordance with the DGCA prescribed Syllabus. We offer a duration of less than 1 year from ground classes till completion of flying.

We have a staff constituted of dynamic and experienced instructors and trainers that ensure that you crack the airline entrance exam in one attempt. We offer a program that for exceeds anything else available in the market and guarantee. Our CPL holding students will be ready for the competitive world of modern aviation.

Whether you are an Indian citizen or a foreign national with an overseas license, all that stands in your way from being part of an exciting adventure in global aviation is a DGCA license conversion with our expert training.
