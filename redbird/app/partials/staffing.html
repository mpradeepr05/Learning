b. For Airlines/Operators
i. Pilots/Simulator flight Instructor(SFI) – 4 line description and prospective advantage of accepting them

Being a Pilot means honing in on quick thinking, adapting to new scenarios, being responsible for the lives of passengers placed under your care. All this requires a good amount of knowledge, lots of practice, and ethics that emanate from good character – at Red Bird Aviation we strive to deliver these qualities through the wealth of knowledge from an experienced faculty, our in-house training facilities, and secure environment where people are free to learn and develop into Pilots. The outcome is that we have delivered professionally competent pilots who can face up to any challenge.

We provide services accommodating Dry simulations and Wet simulations on a range of Aircraft. Our simulation services include training on the Airbus A310, A 320, A 330, A 340, A 350, and A 380. We also provide simulation on the Boeing B-727, B-737, B- 747, and B-787.

Our collaboration extends to other flying schools to encapsulate all the training exercises that can be made available to students in one place – Red Bird Aviation.
